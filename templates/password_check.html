Check The Strenght Of Passwrods




About this tool:


The idea behind this tool is simple. Hackers are always trying to get into our accounts for various reasons. This tool will let you know if your password is easy for a hacker to obtain.


It is widley accepted in the cyber security industry that your password should be atleast 8 characters long and contain numbers and symbols to create a password of minimum security. However using an rtx4090 an attacker could crack a password of this length in under an hour in some circumstances. To combat this I suggest at minimum 10 characters and preferably atleast 16 characters long. This length with a strong random password could take years to crack, making it worthless to hackers.


Making sure your password is completely random and unique for each website and app is just as important as the length of the password. Hackers have many tools and techniques to guess passwords that could be related to you, your loved ones, your hobbies, your city and much more to speed up the cracking process. Your password needs to be unique for each service as everyday there are password databases for sael on the internet that have been obtained from hacked websites. If your password is in this databse and you have used it on multiple websites, the hackers would now have access to all those accounts.


The best and simplest way to have strong and secure passwords is to use a password manager. I recommend Dashlane and 1Password for paid managers and Bitwarden for a free alternative. These apps will create impossible to crack passwords that are unique for each website and app you use, while at the same time remembering them for you and autofilling them for you.
