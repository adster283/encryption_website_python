Check For Weird Characters In Link





About this tool:

Fishing attacks are attacks where the hackers attempt to get individuals to click a link that looks like a service they might use. They then prompt the individual to login to what seems a legitimate sign in page, although this is just a fake page and stores your username and password so the hackers can later use the credentials to login as that individual. The first and simplest technique people are taught to spot these links is to check for misspellings and similar words that might look correct at first glance. This is very effective, however attackers started using similar characters that look identical to the correct characters to the naked eye and even a trained individual would fall for these links.


That's where this tool can help. This tool can spot invisible and similar characters that might trick you into thinking a link is legitimate.
